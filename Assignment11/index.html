To Summarize, "Tangled" is a Disney animated film released in 2010. The movie is a reimagining of the classic fairy tale Rapunzel. It follows the story of Rapunzel, a princess with magical long hair that possesses the power to heal and rejuvenate. She has spent her entire life in a tower, isolated from the outside world by an overprotective witch named Mother Gothel. The plot unfolds when a charming thief named Flynn Rider stumbles upon Rapunzel's tower while fleeing from the authorities. The movie explores themes of self-discovery, freedom, and the transformative power of love. In the end, Rapunzel reunites with her royal parents, and Flynn undergoes a change of heart, ultimately leading to a happy resolution for the characters.
