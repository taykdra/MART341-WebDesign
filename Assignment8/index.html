A Christmas Story Summary

"A Christmas Story" is a classic 1983 set in the 1940s that follows a young boy named Ralphie Parker, who is determined to convince his parents, his teacher, and even Santa Claus to get him a Red Ryder Carbine Action 200-shot Range Model air rifle for Christmas. The film humorously captures the challenges and adventures Ralphie faces, including dealing with bullies, his quirky family dynamics, and various misadventures.
