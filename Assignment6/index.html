T’s Famous Sugar Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 ¼ cups sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
Directions
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degreeso F.
  • Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  • Cream butter and sugar together
  • Add egg and vanilla extract.Mix.
  • Add in flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Combine.
  • Roll dough into small balls, then roll in extra sugar.
  • Bake 9-12 minutes
  • Once time is up, remove from the oven and let sit for 5 minutes. Transfer to a cooling rack.
